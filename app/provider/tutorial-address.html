How to get your own bitcoin address


To broadcast on Streamium, you first need a Bitcoin Wallet to receive the funds. To set this up, we recommend you to use services like blockchain.info, Coinbase or BitGo which are really fast and simple to use.

Once you created your Wallet, you need to provide us with your Bitcoin Address tied to your Bitcoin Wallet. The Address is a unique identifier which allows you to receive bitcoin on your Bitcoin Wallet. The Bitcoin Address is like your email address, and the Bitcoin Wallet the inbox where you receive the emails sent to that address.

The Bitcoin Address should look something like 19ka2SNvry4rocpj1QbJCvwM1vMzBAdqm8.

How to Find Your Bitcoin Address in blockchain.info

If you created a blockchain.info Wallet, you can find your Bitcoin Address here:
  • 1. Log in into blockchain.info.
  • 2. You will find your Bitcoin Address on the bottom left of the screen, below the QR code.
  • 3. Copy your Bitcoin Address.
  • 4. Go to streamium.io, paste the address on the ‘Cashout address’ field and start broadcasting!

How to Find Your Bitcoin Address in Coinbase

If you created a Coinbase Wallet, you can find your Bitcoin Address here:
  • 1. Log in into Coinbase.
  • 2. Click on the ‘Accounts’ section on the left margin of the screen. You should see 2 rows: ‘My Wallet’ and ‘My Vault’.
  • 3. On the ‘My Wallet’ row, click the ‘Obtain Bitcoin Address’ link to the right. You should see a pop-up with a QR code and the Bitcoin Address below.
  • 4. Copy your Bitcoin Address.
  • 5. Go to streamium.io, paste the address on the ‘Cashout address’ field and start broadcasting!

How to Find Your Bitcoin Address in BitGo

If you created a BitGo Wallet, you can find your Bitcoin Address here:
  • 1. Click on the ‘Wallets’ link located on the top center of the screen. You should see a list of all your wallets. If you haven’t created a Wallet yet, you can do it here.
  • 2. Click on your Wallet.
  • 3. Click on the ‘Receive’ button on the right side of the screen, just below the account balance. You should see the Bitcoin Address with a QR code on the right.
  • 4. Copy your Bitcoin Address, which you can find under the ‘Current Receive Address’ title.
  • 5. Go to streamium.io, paste the address on the ‘Cashout address’ field and start broadcasting!
